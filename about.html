About Lavendra Homes

Your trusted free ad posting website for buying, selling, and donating items in Sri Lanka.

Lavendra Homes was created to provide a safe, convenient, and reliable platform for individuals to post free ads across Sri Lanka. Whether it is houses, vehicles, electronics, clothes, or furniture, our goal is to connect buyers and sellers efficiently at no cost.

Our platform is designed with user experience in mind, with simple navigation, easy posting of ads, and clear categories so users can find what they need. We prioritize transparency and trust, ensuring every ad meets our quality standards.

Lavendra Homes is not just a marketplace, it is a community. We encourage donations and sharing, helping people give unused items a new life while supporting those in need. Posting ads on our platform is completely free, making it accessible for everyone.

We continually update and improve the platform to ensure a smooth experience for all users. Your feedback helps us grow and maintain a high-quality free ad posting service.
