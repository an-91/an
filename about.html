About Us

Connecting Sri Lankan businesses with the digital world.

Lavendra Homes was created to provide a safe, convenient, and reliable platform where business owners across Sri Lanka can create free website pages for their businesses.

Our goal is to help entrepreneurs, small businesses, and independent service providers establish an online presence at no cost. Whether you run a shop, offer services, or manage a home-based business, Lavendra Homes allows you to showcase your business details, products, and services through a dedicated business page.

We prioritize transparency, trust, and quality. Every business page follows clear guidelines to ensure accurate information and a professional appearance, creating a trustworthy environment for both businesses and visitors.

Lavendra Homes encourages growth and visibility for local businesses by offering equal opportunity through free business pages. We continually update and improve the platform to ensure a smooth experience for all users, and your feedback helps us grow and maintain a high-quality free business listing service.
