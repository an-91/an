Privacy Policy

Lavendra Homes is committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring a safe online experience.

This Privacy Policy explains how Lavendra Homes collects, uses, and protects the information you provide while using our platform. By using our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, contact number, account credentials, and transaction details. Additionally, we collect usage data and device information to improve the user experience.

Your information is used to provide and improve our services, process transactions, send important notifications, and ensure platform security. Lavendra Homes does not sell your personal information to third parties.

Lavendra Homes may display advertisements from third-party networks. These partners may use cookies or other tracking technologies to serve relevant ads. We encourage reviewing their privacy policies for additional details.

Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.
